Install GougeStop on Your Phone

GougeStop works like an app right from your phone's home screen. No app store needed — just follow the steps below for your phone type.

Why add it to your home screen?

One-tap access. Open GougeStop instantly — no typing a web address or searching through browser tabs.

Use it in the moment. When you're handed an ABN or lab bill at a doctor's office, you want the app ready to go — not buried in a browser.

It works like a regular app. Full screen, fast loading, and it even works offline for pages you've already visited.

1

Open GougeStop in Safari

Go to gougestop.com using the Safari browser. This won't work from Chrome or other browsers on iPhone — it has to be Safari.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap the ☩ share icon at the bottom center of Safari. It's the square with an arrow pointing up.

3

Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"

In the share menu, scroll down until you see "Add to Home Screen." Tap it.

4

Tap "Add"

You'll see a preview with the GougeStop icon and name. Tap "Add" in the top right corner. That's it — GougeStop is now on your home screen.

1

Open GougeStop in Chrome

Go to gougestop.com using Google Chrome. Most Android phones have Chrome pre-installed.

2

Tap the three-dot menu

Tap the ⋮ three dots in the top right corner of Chrome.

3

Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"

Look for "Install app" or "Add to Home screen" in the menu. The wording depends on your phone, but it does the same thing.

4

Confirm the installation

Tap "Install" or "Add" when prompted. GougeStop will appear on your home screen just like any other app.
